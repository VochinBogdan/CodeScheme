  • About the Project

    • About the Project

    Match people to side-projects they wish to work on.

    A website that matches users to available side-projects in the field of computer science, based on a survey of interests and skills. Also allows users to create side-project ideas that can be posted on the website for other users to browse through, or be matched with. Tags are used to easily filter through the different programming languages, skills, and interests. The website also offers an easy way to schedule meetings by taking account the group members’ available times.
